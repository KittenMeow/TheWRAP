Wake Regional Anarchist Project

The Wake Regional Anarchist Project (or the WRAP) will be a project based in Wake County to create an anarchist community. There are many good resources for what anarchism is (many are on the website of the Neighbourhood Anarchist Collective in Eugene, Oregon) and I will write my own later on.

Objectives for the Vision

Summary

Firstly, none of this is final. The vision can always change. I write this to describe what my vision is, but this should ideally be what we envision for the WRAP.

I feel that I should outline some basic objectives of the WRAP. Our highest goal is to achieve anarchism, but there are many steps on that path. One of them is advancing projects that help the community, which will help us show a freer, alternative society based around anarchism.

Additionally, critiquing the systems of control and oppression in current society is extremely important. Doing this requires us to also display a good replacement, which is why we would like to educate about anarchism, both ourselves and the community at large.

Finally, we will be planning for the long-term. We would like to plan for where we will be in the future, and how projects can affect the community later on.


Advancing Projects

The first point that I mentioned is that we will advance projects that help our community. These projects would give people concrete improvements in their lives, as well as transferring power from the powerful to the people.

Projects will be the most important part of the WRAP, helping us fulfill all other objectives.


Critiquing Current Systems

I also said that the WRAP will be critiquing current systems of control and oppression. This will be done via projects and teaching others about these oppressive systems that current society has.


Educating About Anarchism

Education on anarchist thought and critique of the current system are closely linked. Once the oppressive systems such as capitalism, government, and prejudice are dismantled, we need to look into a replacement. The WRAP believes that anarchism is the correct system.

However, people need to understand why anarchism should replace our current system. This will be done with education. The removal of oppressive systems is our first goal, while an anarchist society is our second goal.


Planning for the Long-Term

Finally, we need to plan for the long-term. The WRAP should live even after anarchism is achieved, and we need to understand what we will be doing in the future. Planning for the future is extremely useful for knowing how projects will impact people, preventing a return to oppression, and also showing that we are serious.

Of course, not all plans are good plans. We intend to look critically at everything we do so that we avoid causing issues for the community.

One-Year Vision

Summary

Summary In one year, I hope that the WRAP will have done the following:

- Cleared up misconceptions and mistruths about anarchism.
- Worked on concrete projects that have led to concrete improvements.
- Showed people how life could be better under anarchism.
- Welcomed new members with enthusiasm and support.
- Had effective meetings and decision making.
- Supported diverse communities and formed long-term friendships.
- Planned for the long-term.

Each of these items is extremely important to us. After one year, I hope that the WRAP will have made meaningful steps towards a better community and a better world under anarchism.
